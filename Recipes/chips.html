Classic French Fries

Description:

These crispy fries are seasoned to perfection and fried until golden and crunchy. They make the perfect side dish, snack, or quick comfort bite. Light, crisp on the outside, and fluffy on the inside, these fries pair well with dips like ketchup, BBQ sauce, or mayo. Simple yet delicious, they're great for serving with fried chicken, burgers, or enjoying all on their own.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional seasonings: paprika, garlic powder, or herbs

Instructions:

  1. Wash and peel the potatoes. Cut them into thin, even strips (about 1/4 inch thick).
  2. Soak the cut potatoes in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch. This helps achieve a crispier fry.
  3. Drain and pat the potatoes dry with paper towels.

  5. Fry the potatoes in batches to avoid overcrowding. Cook for about 3-4 minutes per batch until they are light golden and tender. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  6. Increase the oil temperature to 375째F (190째C). Fry the potatoes again in batches for an additional 2-3 minutes until they are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the fries and drain on fresh paper towels. Immediately season with salt and any optional seasonings while still hot.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauces and enjoy!
